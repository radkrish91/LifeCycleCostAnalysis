About

LCCA is one of the most recognized economic evaluation and decision making techniques that can be performed to find optimum strategies of any maintenance and rehabilitation project that take into consideration the life cycle cost of the entire project. This web-based user friendly tool will incorporate a flexible interface to perform LCCA methodology for different scenarios and can access state-wide infrastructure data.
